New Rhino exhibit coming in March

We at BrushWind are happy to announce that we are welcoming a brand new member into our family.
We have purchased a rare White Rhino from a sanctuary in Zimbabwe, South Africa. Ryan the Rhino was rescued as a child after his mother was killed by ivory poachers.
Thanks to our generous donors we are able to welcome Ryan into a home full of staff that will love and care for him for the rest of his life.

Merry-go-Round set to be installed

BrushWind will be hosting out first ride. We will be opening a merry-go-round in July near the front of the park.
We listened carefully to customer feedback and decided that a ride would be a great addition to the park. We are excited to hear how you like the new ride and are open to adding more in the future.

Memo: Please do not feed the animals

We have noticed that some visitors seem to be throwing food into the exhibits.
Please do not do this are animals are already well feed and human food could make them sick. Please do not feed the animals any outside food
food can be purchased at various kiosks around the zoo. Thank you.
