BrushWind Zoo

Welcome to BrushWind Zoo! Established in 2002. We are home to a wide variety of animals. We hope that you and your family come and visit us and experience the best zoo in the world. We also have a delicious restaurant and unlimited free soft drinks!

Conservation Efforts

Our zoo engages in many conservation activities we work with animal sanctuaries to rescue neglected animals from poor living conditions and rehabilitate them in hopes of eventually return them to the wild. We encourage you to donate to our efforts if you are able.
